The numbers of fines imposed are the lowest in the three years of the current administration, in a historical series started in 2013, the oldest data sent by the institute in a FOIA request.

2017: Former president Temer signed a decree that converts environmental fine into services

2019: Despite historic deforestation record, Bolsonaro says he "will end the fine industry”

2020: IBAMA lost half of its inspectors in a decade

2021: Even with an increase in fires, the government applied the fewest number of environmental fines in 20 years
