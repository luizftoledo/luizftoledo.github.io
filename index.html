Investigative Reporting

BBC News Brasil Investigative Series

The Epstein-Brazil connection

I analyzed all the millions of Epstein files released by the US government and built scrapers that searched for keywords related to Brazilian figures. This way, we were able to reveal the financial dependency of Brazilian models on the billionaire, connections used to lure young women, and the victims who were taken to the US. The BBC series led to a Federal Prosecution investigation in Brazil seeking a network that lured women. Combined, the reporting reached over 10 million views in text and video and resonated across all major Brazilian media outlets.

The English version of the investigation — Epstein used modelling agent to recruit girls, Brazilian women tell BBC — was published internationally and translated into several languages, including French (Epstein utilisait un agent de mannequins pour recruter des jeunes filles, témoignent des Brésiliennes auprès de la BBC), Polish (Brazylijskie modelki twierdzą, że Epstein rekrutował młode dziewczyny przez agenta modelek), Serbian (Brazilke za BBC: Epstin koristio modnog agenta za regrutaciju devojaka), Chinese (巴西女性向BBC透露，爱泼斯坦曾利用模特儿经纪人招募女孩), and Ukrainian (Як модельні агенції вербували неповнолітніх дівчат для Епштейна).
